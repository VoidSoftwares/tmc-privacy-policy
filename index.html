Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how the TMC Masjid App ("App") collects, uses, and discloses your information.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information from you:

  • Device Information: We may collect information about your device, such as your device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and IP address.
  • Location Information: We may collect your location information to provide you with accurate prayer times. You can control your location permissions in your device settings.
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you use the App, such as the features you access and the frequency of your use.
  • Donation Information: If you choose to donate through the App, we may collect your payment information, such as your name and credit card details. This information is collected by a secure payment processor and is not stored by the App.

Use of Information

We may use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve the App's features, such as providing accurate prayer times and sending adhan reminders.
  • Send you push notifications about prayer times, events, and announcements.
  • Personalize your experience with the App.
  • Process your donations.
  • Analyze how the App is used.

Disclosure of Information

We may disclose your information to third-party service providers who help us operate the App, such as analytics providers and payment processors. These third-party service providers are obligated to keep your information confidential and secure.

We may also disclose your information if we are required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such disclosure is necessary to protect our rights or the rights of others.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the security of your information.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe that your child has provided us with information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove the information from our servers.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on the App.

About Us

TMC Masjid - Your All-in-One Islamic Companion App

At TMC Masjid, we believe that technology can be a powerful tool to deepen your faith and connect you to your Islamic community. That's why we created the TMC Masjid App, the ultimate resource for Muslims on the go.

Our app offers a comprehensive suite of features designed to enrich your spiritual life and provide essential Islamic guidance, all in one convenient place. Whether you're a seasoned practitioner or just beginning your Islamic journey, TMC Masjid is here to support you every step of the way.

Our Purpose

  • Empower your practice: We provide the tools you need to perform your daily prayers, recite Quran, learn from Hadiths and Azkar, and calculate Zakat.
  • Deepen your knowledge: Explore the vast wisdom of Islam with access to the Quran, Islamic lectures through Makkah Live, and an Islamic AI Chatbot for faith-based questions.
  • Stay connected: Find mosques near you with our Mosque Locator, receive Daily Prayer Notifications to stay on track, and personalize your device with beautiful Islamic Wallpapers.
  • Embrace community: Through our app, we hope to foster a sense of connection and belonging within the Muslim community.

Multilingual Support

TMC Masjid app caters to a global audience, offering multilingual support in English, Arabic, Turkish, and Urdu.

We are committed to providing a user-friendly and enriching experience for Muslims of all backgrounds. Download TMC Masjid today and embark on a journey of spiritual growth and connection.
